Hey, i’m Stephanie Schneider.

Highly adaptable, passionate, keen on details, team player.

I am a recent University of California San Diego graduate: B.S. Cognitive Science; Specialization in Design Interaction.

Before transferring to San Diego, I worked 3.5 years in a flourishing visual department within a marketing startup.

I am currently interested in:

-Product Design

-Visual Communication Design

-User Experience Design

-User Interface Design

Call me Steph!

Designing is my flow state. Detail oriented; trained to see the big picture. I value the potential of a strong team dynamic.

I generally find myself considering potential redesigns for fun. What tiny detail might improve this product? How might I have improved the product if I helped design it? If a designer doesn’t consider the fine details, users will likely be asking these same questions later on.

Aesthetics and functionality enable design, good design sparks joy . I have learned that design isn’t about the product, but the people who will experience it. Human-Centered Design is meant to bridge these [product & users] successfully. I believe that including users within the ideation and prototyping processes is crucial.

I also believe creating interfaces with seamless functionality is absolutely essential.
